Michael Matthews
I grew up in a small rural town in Ohio, moved to New Orleans for five years, and then moved back to a suburb in northern Cincinnati. Living in New Orleans has given me a unique perspective on life and offered experiences that I will never forget, but I have found that hurricanes are much less damaging when they reach Ohio than they are when they strike the Gulf Coast.

I have always had a passion for computers and have turned that passion into an 8 year IT career. In 2011 I made the decision to return to school to pursue a Bachelor Degree in Information Technology with a concentration in Software Development. I currently maintain a 3.54 GTA and plan to graduate in 2015. I plan to use the knowledge that I have acquired and am currently learning in order to move from a support role into application development.

When I am not busy at work or studying, I enjoy cooking, cycling, and running. I am interested in learning to play the guitar, but it would be a large understatement to say that I am bad at it. I maintain a positive attitude about this by reminding myself that there is nowhere for my guitar playing skills to go but up!
